JavaScript features from Milestone 5

  • Move the logo back and forth from one top corner to the other, on the HOME and ABOUT ME pages.
  • Stop the logo animation and display a square logo on the POWERLIFTING VIDEOS and PHOTO GALLERY pages.

PATHFINDER

Welcome to my little JS game I call "Pathfinder"! This micro-game only has one level, as I have developed it purely for this school project website.

How to play: The goal is to reveal the hidden path from start to finish by clicking a tile adjacent to the last tile you clicked.
If the tile is part of the path, it will turn white. If it is not, the board will reset. You'll know if you've finished. Begin by clicking a tile!
(Hint: the first one is to the right of the first white tile.)
